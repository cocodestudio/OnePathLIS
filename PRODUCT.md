# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Laboratory Technicians, Pathologists, and Admins who need to register patients, log diagnostic data, review reports, and manage billing in a fast-paced environment.

## Product Purpose
OnePath Lab is a multi-tenant Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) built to streamline clinical intake, diagnostic test verification, and automated invoicing. Success looks like zero manual result entry errors, high contrast for readability under lab fluorescent lights, and instant PDF clinical reports.

## Brand Personality
Precise, Professional, Reliable, and Reassuring. 3-word personality: Clinical, Streamlined, Trustworthy.

## Anti-references
Cluttered, hard-to-read enterprise database software. Saturated, overstimulating neon dashboards. Saturated warm cream backgrounds (avoiding standard AI cream aesthetic).

## Design Principles
1. **Absolute Clinical Contrast**: All critical laboratory metrics and diagnostic values must be immediately readable with high contrast, complying with WCAG AA.
2. **Minimal Cognitive Load**: Group diagnostics clearly (e.g. by parameter type) to prevent technician data entry fatigue.
3. **Structured Flow**: Match physical lab procedures—from patient intake, to test execution, to pathologist sign-off.
4. **Professional Dignity**: Clinical reports and layouts must look authoritative and clean, ensuring patient and doctor confidence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA contrast compliance.
- Keyboard navigability for rapid data entry fields.
- Reduced motion support for chart and transition animations.
